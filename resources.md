### [Blogging Workshop](https://github.com/notanotherpizza/blog-workshop)

Writing blogs can be beneficial to technical careers and is a great way to learn new things and get better at writing, but where to start?

This workshop covers the why, what, and how of writing blogs: digging into the benefits of blogging, how to find topics to write about, tips for the writing process, and how to get your work out there.

Participants will leave with an outline of a blog that could be used to create a draft of their first blog. Participants will also get first-hand experience of creating a blog using Markdown and Jekyll, and will raise a pull request to contribute their work to a shared repository.
